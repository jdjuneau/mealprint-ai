## For Web-Only Users

### Option 1: Fitbit (Recommended)
1. Go to `/health-tracking`
2. Click "Connect" next to Fitbit
3. Authorize in Fitbit's OAuth screen
4. Done! Data syncs every hour automatically

### Option 2: Strava (For Workouts)
1. Go to `/health-tracking`
2. Click "Connect" next to Strava
3. Authorize in Strava's OAuth screen
4. Done! Workouts sync every hour automatically

### Option 3: Bluetooth Device
1. Go to `/health-tracking`
2. Click "Connect Bluetooth Device"
3. Select your fitness tracker/watch
4. Data syncs every minute automatically
